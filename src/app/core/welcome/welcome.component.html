What's Studybuddy?
Studybuddy is a community of students looking for peers to study with.
Search for your classes and see who's looking for a study buddy in them,
so you can skip the hassle of studying alone and get to studying together.

Benefits
Buddy system
If you don't get something, having someone clear up your confusion saves time that you would have spent trying to figure out difficult concepts.
Avoid procrastinating
Meet with a classmate to work on assignments and study for exams. Help each other stay focused while studying and on-time with deadlines.
Share knowledge
Get a better grasp of the subject by sharing notes and understandings of the topic. Remember, two heads are better than one.
